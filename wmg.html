Competitive Analysis: (literature review & competitor analysis) •lot of roommate finding apps •money apps: venmo, split wise, tricount •General tasks: Roof, Tody •house tasks: flatastic •familychores: Our Home, cozi, homey Flatastic: In German even after language change, UI not good, hard to read. Has a scoring system, side tab, with notifications. Our Home: has some info fill out, email required, homepage is tasks to do, with people displayed across top. bottom nav. Option to repeat tasks. Also has a points/rewards system. Has option to change icons for new tasks, and for people to attach photos. Theres a points bank as well- mainly to reward kids. Can see self completed tasks. Also has groceries as separate task tab. Can see the tasks you completed-can also move back to “to-do”. Can also assign tasks (a little complicated) Can’t edit made tasks though nudge function is nice. People want a notepad or non task related things (reminders) Also the ability to hide appoints/lists Accessibility- option for bigger text. some small icons are hard to click/not as high up on the hierarchy as they should be Easy to add new task (all apps mostly have in same place) Theres option to sort tasks by type. I think its necessary to have some sort of chat. maybe have a “bulletin board” of all new feed/updates have a nice loading page Cozi: also has initial info ask. Also has option to integrate calendars (google, apple, outlook). Homepage is also tasks but in calendar. a little redundant on bottom nav (yes, bottom nav as well.). Also a family oriented app. UI not as good as Our Home UI is bulky Homey: good UI, also has initial screening(invite members etc), household creation. They have a chat screen too many unnecessary/irrelevant features They have a left side nav as well as a bottom nav.. Their tasks/chores are predestined- but I don’t think its that useful bc you can edit it later. You can also add a picture. Also no option just to have it general-not assigned to anyone (like a reminder) (there’s too much explanation for everything in genera) Responsibility= “dashboard” where you can see everyone’s chores Roof: phone number verification is annoying. I like their nav. Some settings stuff straight off the bat. (food and bad) You also have the ability to pin things! They have an activity thread as well I like the simplicity/ease of adding a task. Their one screen seems to have everything.
